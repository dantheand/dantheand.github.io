Current research and interests

Control Theory Applications in Synthetic Biology

My current work has been in applying control theory design paradigms to genetic circuit design. Control theory provides a powerful analytical framework for dynamical systems. I am currently using it, in conjuction with MATLAB simulations, to devise new, robust genetic circuits for synthetic biological applications.

New Synthetic Biology Tools and Technologies

The nascent engineering field of synthetic biology has many unexplored spacess. I am particularly interested in several: analog computation in genetic circuits, multiplexing fluorescent protein output, and creating new and expanding upon current measurement standards.
